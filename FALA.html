FALA magazine spread

This project was created for an assignment in a university course called foundatrions of grafic design. The assignment was to create a spread in a magazine or coffe table book about any kind of designer. I chose to make it about the architecture firm FALA since I found the images of their buildings to be both inspiring in their design and easy to design around.
